Whales

Whales are a diverse group of aquatic placental marine mammals. They belong to the Cetartiodactyle order like dolphins and porpoises. They normally have a life span of 10-45 years. Their speed ranges 29 mph to 35 mph.

Blue Whale is a marine mammal and a baleen whale. It is the largest animal that's known to have ever existed. They weigh from 290,000 to 330,000 lbs. They have a life span of 80-90 years. They are considered an endangered species. Learn more here
